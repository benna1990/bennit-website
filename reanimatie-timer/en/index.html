Features

What CPR Timer
does for you.

Designed for the field. Minimal interaction, maximum support.

Smart CPR timer

Automatically tracks total resuscitation time and current set time. Visible at a glance:

  • How long the resuscitation has been running
  • When the next set starts
  • When a rhythm check is coming up

Shock registration

Log shocks with a single tap. The app keeps track of:

  • Total number of shocks delivered
  • Exact time each shock was given
  • Which set it occurred in

Automatic sets & cycles

Sets, rotation moments and progress are tracked automatically — no manual counting during the call.

  • Set count tracked
  • Rotation moments logged
  • Structure during long incidents

Voice alerts

Audio cues so you look at the screen less:

  • "At 1 minute" (halfway)
  • "15 seconds remaining"
  • "Rhythm check / rotate"

Clear session log

A full overview after the incident for handover, evaluation and debriefing:

  • Timeline of events
  • Shocks, sets and duration
  • Ideal for reporting

Metronome (100–120 BPM)

Built-in metronome for correct compression rate. Adjustable from 100 to 120 BPM, in line with adult and paediatric CPR guidelines.

  • Adjustable 100–120 BPM
  • Includes reference to paediatric CPR protocol (PDF in app)
  • Total time adjustable for delays

FAQ

Everything you want to know.

Is the CPR Timer app free?
Yes, the app is free to download from the App Store. The timer, metronome and all voice alerts always work for free — without limits during a real call. The free version stores the last 3 sessions in the log. An organisation license unlocks the full session history.
What does CPR Timer actually do?
The app provides: automatic time tracking of total CPR time and current set time, shock logging with timestamp and set number, voice alerts at rotation moments and rhythm checks, an adjustable metronome (100–120 BPM), and a clear log for handover to the ambulance or debriefing.
Who is the app designed for?
The app is designed for professional first responders: fire departments, emergency response teams, first aid associations, EMS/paramedics and CPR trainers. Also ideal for training evenings, drills and exams.
Does the app work without internet?
Yes, the core functionality works fully offline — even on scene without signal. Session data is stored exclusively on the device. No personal data is collected. The app sends fully anonymous usage statistics on each launch (no device ID, no email, no session data). This can be disabled via Settings → Privacy.
Which devices are supported?
CPR Timer is available for iPhone and iPad running iOS 17.5 or later. Download free from the Apple App Store — 10.4 MB, no account required.
